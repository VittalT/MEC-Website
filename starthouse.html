Starthouse

​​The Starthouse is an initiative designed for student founders at MIT to kickstart their entrepreneurial journey and go from idea to launch in a four-week program. The program includes workshops, networking sessions, and direct mentorship from seasoned entrepreneurs and investors, as well as a trip to San Francisco where founders are able to meet with investors and share what they are working on.

If interested in learning more, reach out to mec-exec@mit.edu.
