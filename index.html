MIT's Undergraduate Community of Founders

About MEC

MEC is a community of founders and an interface for students who are looking to work at startups. Our mission is to empower MIT undergraduates to dream big and innovate.

Our minds are influenced by the people and events around us. We believe that building a community of aspiring entrepreneurs supports and inspires students to actively pursue their passions.

Our Goals

Bring together exceptional builders and give them the resources and network to build a company with real users and revenue

Connect students with current founders and MIT alum

Connect MIT students with other student founders in the Boston area and around the country

Pair students with startups for summer internships or full time work

Connect student founders with the right resources to set them up for success (i.e. VCs, funding, legal resources, etc.)

Foster a spirit of innovation and entrepreneurship on campus by giving students the resources they need to build their vision
