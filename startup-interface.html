Startup Interface

About

Startup Interface is a new initiative by MEC designed to connect students with promising startups that are actively recruiting. We aim to serve as a bridge between the wider entrepreneurial community and entrepreneurially-minded students, providing them with invaluable experience, networking opportunities, and an opportunity to work with innovative, fast-growing companies.

Startups

We accept startup sponsors on a rolling basis. If you’re a startup and looking to recruit MIT students, reach out to us at mec-startup@mit.edu for more information.
